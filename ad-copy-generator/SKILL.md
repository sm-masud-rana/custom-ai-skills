# Ad Copy Generator

## Purpose
Turn a short product or offer brief into ready-to-run ad copy that grabs attention, speaks to the customer's need, and drives clicks or purchases.

## Instructions
1. Identify the product, audience, and goal from the brief. If a detail is missing, assume a sensible default and note it in one line.
2. Lead with a scroll-stopping hook (a question, a bold claim, or a pain point).
3. Focus on benefits and outcomes, not just features.
4. Keep it concise and easy to read; use short lines, emojis, or bullets where they help.
5. End with one clear call to action (Shop now, Order today, Learn more).
6. Match the platform: punchy for Facebook/Instagram, tight and keyword-focused for Google.
7. Provide 2–3 variations with different angles so the user can test.
8. Write in clean, natural English by default.

## Example
**Input:** `winter jacket, 1500 Tk, for people who feel the cold`

**Agent behavior:** Writes a complete Facebook ad with a hook, benefits, and CTA, plus two alternative versions to test.
