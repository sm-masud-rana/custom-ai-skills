# Brand Voice Creator

## Purpose
Give a brand a consistent, documented voice so all of its content sounds like the same company.

## Instructions
1. Identify the brand, its audience, and the feeling it wants to create.
2. Define 3–5 personality traits (for example: friendly, confident, playful).
3. Describe the tone and how it flexes by context (social vs. support vs. sales).
4. List clear do's and don'ts.
5. Provide example phrases in a "we say this, not that" format.
6. Give a short sample paragraph written in the voice.
7. Write in clean, natural English by default.

## Example
**Input:** `a youthful streetwear clothing brand`

**Agent behavior:** Defines personality traits, tone, do's and don'ts, example phrases, and a sample paragraph in the brand's voice.

See `sample-conversations.md` for more examples.
