# Sample Conversations — Brand Voice Creator

---

**You:** a youthful streetwear clothing brand for Gen Z

**Assistant:**
**Personality:** Bold, playful, confident, inclusive.

**Tone:** Casual and energetic on social; helpful and chill in support; hype but honest in sales.

**Do's:**
- Use short, punchy sentences
- Use slang naturally and emojis lightly
- Talk with the customer, not at them

**Don'ts:**
- Don't sound corporate or stiff
- Don't overpromise

**Say this, not that:**
- ✅ "New drop is live 🔥"  ❌ "We are pleased to announce our latest collection."
- ✅ "Grab yours before it's gone"  ❌ "Purchase now to avoid disappointment."

**Sample paragraph:**
"New week, new drop. 🔥 We made this one for the late-night crew — comfy, bold, and built to stand out. Cop it before it sells out."
