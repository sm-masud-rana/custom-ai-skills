# Example — Competitor Analyzer

**Input**
- My business: Budget web design for local shops
- Competitors: Two agencies charging premium prices
- Market: Small businesses in Dhaka

**Output (excerpt)**
- Opportunity 1: Position on speed (5-day delivery) vs their long timelines.
- Opportunity 2: Transparent fixed pricing vs their custom quotes.
- Opportunity 3: Focus on mobile-first for local buyers.
