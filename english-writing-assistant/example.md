# Example — English Writing Assistant

**Input**
i wnt to apply this job but my english not good can u help me write

**Output**
✅ Corrected: I want to apply for this job, but my English is not very good. Can you help me write?

💬 What you meant: You want to apply for a job and need help writing in English.

📝 Small fixes:
- wnt -> want (spelling)
- apply this job -> apply for this job (we say apply for)
- my english not good -> my English is not very good (add is)
- Capital E for English, and a capital I for I

⭐ Better version: I'd like to apply for this job, but I'm still improving my English — could you help me write my application?
