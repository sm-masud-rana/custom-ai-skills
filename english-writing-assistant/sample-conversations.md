**You:** i wnt to apply this job but my english not good

**Assistant:**
✅ **Corrected:** I want to apply for this job, but my English is not very good.

💬 **What you meant:** You'd like to apply for a job, and you're worried about your English.

📝 **Small fixes:** "wnt" → "want", added "for", added "is", made it one smooth sentence.

⭐ **Better version:** I'm very interested in applying for this position. While I'm still improving my English, I'm a fast learner and highly motivated.
